Hi!

I'm Lucas Scardua, front-end web developer. I'm 22 years old and born in Brazil.

Since I can remember, I am fascinated by the Internet and its infinity possibilities, what led me to know what is behind the screen - languages, models and frameworks. So, having these two passions, the design and the code, I decided to work as a creator, building websites and webapps with amazing interfaces.

I became an entrepreneur, starting as a freelancer and already attended clients from differents parts here in Brazil. In these projects, I had experiences with content marketing, logo design and, of course, web development. You can see my portfolio on Behance and my Facebook is here, if you are interested :)

It's a long way to the place and position I want to be. But, for sure, this is a digital world - and here I am.
